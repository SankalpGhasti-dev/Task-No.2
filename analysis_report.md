# Phishing Email Analysis Report

## Sample Overview
The email claims to be from "People’s Bank" and informs the recipient about a payment being processed. It attempts to trick the user into verifying the transaction.

---

## Phishing Indicators Identified

### 1. Fake Sender Identity
The email claims to be from People’s Bank without providing a verified official domain.

---

### 2. Urgency & Financial Pressure
The message mentions a payment of $495.42 to create panic and force quick action.

---

### 3. Suspicious Instructions
The user is asked to click a removed URL and login to verify payment details — a common phishing tactic.

---

### 4. Generic Greeting
No personalized customer name is used.

---

### 5. Inconsistent Language
Multiple grammatical inconsistencies and unnatural wording are present.

---

### 6. No Reply Allowed
The email states replies are not monitored — another phishing red flag.

---

## Conclusion
This email contains multiple phishing characteristics such as fake sender identity, financial urgency, suspicious links, and poor language. It is clearly a phishing attempt and should be deleted or reported.
